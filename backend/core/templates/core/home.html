Areas of Bali

Explore our available villas in Bali's most rapidly developing areas

  • Canggu
  • Bukit
  • Ubud

Canggu is one of Bali's most vibrant districts, famous for its world-class surf breaks, trendy cafés, and buzzing nightlife. As the epicenter of modern island living, it attracts digital nomads, expats, and investors alike. With a booming real estate market and strong demand for short-term rentals, Canggu offers some of the highest returns on the island. It's the perfect choice for active travelers and those looking for reliable passive income.

The Bukit Peninsula is renowned for its stunning clifftop views, pristine beaches, and luxury resorts. This area has become increasingly popular among high-end property investors seeking premium ocean-view villas and exclusive residential communities. With world-famous beaches like Uluwatu and Padang Padang nearby, Bukit offers a sophisticated lifestyle combined with excellent investment potential.

Ubud represents the cultural heart of Bali, surrounded by lush rice terraces, ancient temples, and traditional villages. This area attracts those seeking a more peaceful, spiritual lifestyle away from the coastal buzz. Investment opportunities here focus on boutique villas, eco-resorts, and wellness retreats, appealing to a growing market of conscious travelers and long-term residents seeking tranquility and authentic Balinese culture.

Leasehold in Bali is a long-term lease, typically 25–30 years, often with options to extend (e.g. 25+25+25). The land remains owned by an Indonesian, while the foreign investor gains the right to use and rent the property. It is similar to long-term leasing in Europe, but with decades-long terms.
